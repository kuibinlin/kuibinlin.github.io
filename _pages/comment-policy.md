I genuinely enjoy hearing from readers — whether you think I've got something wrong, know a better way to do it, or just want to share what worked for you. Comments are open for that reason.

That said, a few house rules.

### Keep it decent

Disagree all you like — I'm fine with that. Just keep it about the topic, not the person. If you wouldn't say it to someone's face at a meetup, probably leave it out.

### What's always welcome

- Pointing out mistakes (seriously, please do)
- Sharing a different approach or tool you've used
- Asking questions — no question is too basic here

### What gets removed

- Anything rude or hostile
- Spam and self-promotion
- Links dropped without context

### A couple of practicalities

Comments with links go into a moderation queue first. Threads close after 7 days. If you keep causing trouble, you'll get quietly removed.

That's about it. Thanks for taking the time to read and comment — it means a lot.
